Snowman Game (2018)

Using Processing3, I coded a 2D game where you, as the player, play as a snowman who doesn't like children very much. You don't like the sun all that much either. The objective is to survive for as long as you can before the children or the rays of sunlight do you in.

Use the WASD keys to move left and right and the SPACEBAR to jump.

You can also use your mouse to left click and throw snowballs at incoming children for self defense.
